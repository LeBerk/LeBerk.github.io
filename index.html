The Rêverie Cigars Show

Stories worth
slowing down
to hear

In a world of instant content and fleeting attention, Rêverie is built around the idea that the best conversations happen slowly — like a cigar that rewards patience.

We sit down with the makers, masters, and visionaries of the cigar world to go beyond the leaf. Behind every great brand is a human story — an origin, a struggle, a philosophy. That's what we're here to uncover.

"In a world where everything is instant, cigars are one of the only things left to bring us down to earth."
Berk, Founder — Rêverie Society

Hosted by certified cigar sommelier and entrepreneur Berk, the show brings a young founder's curiosity to an ancient craft — asking the questions that matter: How did you start? What nearly broke you? What does this industry mean to you?

Our Purpose

We believe the stories behind the smoke are as rich and complex as the cigars themselves. Rêverie exists to tell them.

The Host

Berk

Founder · Certified Cigar Sommelier

At 16, over two Romeo y Juliettas brought straight from Cuba, Berk had a conversation that changed everything. A successful entrepreneur across the table told stories from South America — and in the slow burn of that evening, ambition took on a new shape.

Before Rêverie, Berk built businesses from age 13, helped take a startup to a $2.5M VC-backed exit, and hosted a podcast interviewing figures like Geoff Ralston, former president of Y Combinator, alongside early investors in Facebook, Pinterest, Lyft, and Palantir. He earned his sommelier certification through Tobacconist University and interviewed its founder, Jorge Armenteros — the man behind Episode 01.

He started Rêverie because he believes cigars are one of the last things that force us to slow down — and in that slowness, something true is found.
